About Me

Hello, my name is Nurul Atieqah binti Sa'ari, a 21-year-old individual navigating through life's intricate tapestry. Born into a family of six, I find myself situated as the third child among my four siblings. Each day brings new experiences, and amidst them, I discover joy in the simple pleasures of life. My palate dances with delight at the thought of nasi goreng chicken chop, a dish that never fails to satisfy my culinary cravings. Accompanied by copious amounts of coffee, my beverage of choice, these flavors weave into the fabric of my daily life. Beyond the realm of gastronomy, I find solace in the laughter and playfulness of children, an aspect of life that brings warmth to my heart.Embedded in the very core of my being is a profound love for learning. The pursuit of knowledge is not a chore but a journey I eagerly embark upon. Whether it's delving into the pages of a new book, engaging in thought-provoking discussions, or immersing myself in the diverse world of information available, the joy derived from learning is a constant companion in my life. This enthusiasm transcends academic boundaries, influencing not only my education but also shaping the way I approach challenges and opportunities.
